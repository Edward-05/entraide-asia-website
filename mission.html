Our Mission

To close the gap between generosity and impact.

We exist to make sure every act of giving reaches the people who need it — with dignity, accountability and lasting results.

Our Pillars

Four commitments behind every program we run

1

Direct Impact

98% of funds go straight to programs — never to overhead or unnecessary admin.

2

Local First

Every program is designed and led by people from the community it serves.

3

Open Reporting

Quarterly financial audits and program updates are published for anyone to review.

4

Sustainable Growth

We build multi-year partnerships designed to outlast any single funding cycle.

Our Approach

How we turn resources into durable change

  • ✓ Partner with vetted, community-led organizations on the ground.
  • ✓ Co-design programs with the people they're meant to serve.
  • ✓ Fund in multi-year cycles so progress isn't interrupted.
  • ✓ Measure outcomes, not just activity — and publish what we find.
  • ✓ Support small, locally-run schools in Vietnam, Cambodia and Thailand, and individual students in Myanmar.
