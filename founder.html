Founder's Story

From a career in business to a mission for children across Southeast Asia.

Christophe Goisset, a retired businessman from Bordeaux, France, founded Entraide Asia to put his savings and his time to work for children who simply want to stay in school.

Bordeaux to Ho Chi Minh City

A retirement spent giving back

After a long career in business, Christophe Goisset retired with a simple idea: rather than let his savings sit idle, he wanted to put them to direct use for children who needed it most. A visit to Maison Chance, a school and shelter for children with disabilities in Ho Chi Minh City, turned that idea into a plan. He founded Entraide Asia in early 2025 to fund what the school needed most — safer classrooms, accessibility ramps, and the everyday supplies that keep a child coming back the next day.

What began as support for a single school has since grown into partnerships across four countries, still run the same way Christophe started it: modestly, transparently, and with the people closest to each child making the decisions.

Where His Support Goes

Three ways Christophe's fund helps children stay in school

◆

Scholarships

Tuition, books and daily costs for promising students at Bayon School in Cambodia and for individually sponsored students in Myanmar, so financial hardship doesn't end an education.

●

School Facilities

Classroom renovations and accessibility ramps at Maison Chance in Vietnam, and basic supplies and repairs for the small schools and shelters Entraide partners with.

▲

Local Partnerships

Funding for vocational and life-skills training at Living Grace in Thailand, always channelled through local staff and teachers who know each community best.

The Story So Far

How the fund has grown since 2025

Early 2025

Entraide Asia is founded

Christophe Goisset commits personal funds to support classrooms and staff at Maison Chance in Vietnam.

Mid 2025

First partnership in Cambodia

Funding begins for scholarships and school supplies at Bayon School near Siem Reap.

Late 2025

Transparency reporting launched

Christophe insists on publishing quarterly independent audits of every program the fund supports.

Early 2026

Expanded to Thailand and Myanmar

Support begins for Living Grace Enhance Skills School and individual student sponsorships in Myanmar.

Today

Still hands-on

Christophe remains closely involved in the fund's work, reviewing each program's results directly with local partners.

"I spent my career building a business. I'd rather spend my retirement making sure a child in Vietnam or Cambodia doesn't have to choose between school and everything else their family needs."

Christophe Goisset Founder, Entraide Asia — Bordeaux, France
