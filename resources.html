FAQ

Common questions about our work

How is Entraide funded?

Entraide is funded through a mix of foundation grants, corporate partnerships and individual supporters, with all financials disclosed in our quarterly audits.

How do you choose which communities to work with?

We partner with communities that invite us in, prioritizing locations where local organizations are already leading the response.

Is Entraide a registered nonprofit?

Yes — Entraide Asia is a registered nonprofit association (loi 1901) based in France.

How often do you report on impact?

We publish independently audited financial and program reports every quarter, available on this Resources page.
