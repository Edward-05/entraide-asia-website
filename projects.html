Small schools making a big difference across Southeast Asia

Entraide proudly partners with several small, locally-run schools and shelters that open the door to education for children who would otherwise be left behind.

Ongoing · Vietnam

Maison Chance

Based in Ho Chi Minh City, Maison Chance has offered a home, medical care, education and vocational training to orphaned and disabled children and adults since 1993 — a genuine springboard toward independence.

Learn More →
Ongoing · Cambodia

Bayon School

Near Siem Reap, Bayon School provides free primary and secondary education to children from disadvantaged families, along with school materials, daily meals and scholarships to continue on to university.

Learn More →
Ongoing · Thailand

Living Grace Enhance Skills School

This small community school helps underserved children stay in the classroom, building foundational skills and confidence alongside their education.
